The Indolence of the Filipino People

José Rizal

"The Indolence of the Filipino People" (1890) by José Rizal argues that Filipino "laziness" is a result of Spanish oppression, poor governance, and lack of opportunities, not natural traits. He calls for reforms to restore productivity and progress.
